Creation of Jorge Enterprises

in 1955, Jorge, together with the help of Bruno Madrigal, started a spackle business for the Encanto. When Jorge stepped down in 1956 to CFO, Bruno took over as CEO and expanded to past the Encanto. It did not do that well, until a sudden spike in sales. At this point, Bruno realised that spackles where well needed and he made more. In the summer of 1958, Jorge Enterprises became international, reaching Venezuela and Ecuador. It wouldn't take long until Jorge Enterprises became worldwide.

Jorge Enterprises' impact on the world

Jorge Enterprises' impact on the world is drastic. About 15 spackles get sold monthly. Many people believe in the Jorge Enterprises brand and trust in only the best quality there is to offer.

What Jorge Enterprises does with its money

Jorge Enterprises pledges all the money will go into helping, whether it be Bruno's family, the Encanto, Colombia, the world, or even just an old lady wanting to cross the road. The company will never spend extra money for unnecessary stuff, and will all go to making the world a better place.
