About Jorge Enterprises' Creation

Jorge Enterprises is a company based in Colombia, in the Encanto. It was founded on June 7, 1955, when Jorge (friend of Bruno Madrigal) decided to sell his custom-made spackles to the world. Since Jorge's Retirement in 1956, Bruno Madrigal became the CEO of the enterprise. As of now, Jorge Enterprises® is the 145th spackle making business in all of Colombia, and 862nd in the world.
