About Jorge

Jorge, maker of the spackle, is the founder of our enterprise. Jorge met Bruno Madrigal when Bruno lived in the walls. During the time in the walls, Bruno needed to fix the cracks, so he hired Hernando to patch them and hired Jorge to make the spackle. Since then, Jorge's spackles became better and better. After the collapse of the Casa Madrigal, Jorge created the spackles necessary to re-build it. Since then, Jorge kept on-upping his spackles, and even today, we still strive to make the best spackles.

Jorge Right Now

Jorge is currently the CFO of the company, and lives a happy life with his pet rat, Alejandro, (given to him as a gift for his birthday by Bruno). He has bonded with his best-friend's family, especially Bruno's youngest nephew.
